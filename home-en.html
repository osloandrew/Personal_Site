About Me

Hi! My name is Andrew, and I'm a Junior Mendix Consultant at CLEVR.

I come from the town of Brookline, Massachusetts, USA. When I was in high school, I developed a deep passion for languages. I learned French, Latin, and German through classes and Norwegian on my own.

I studied Political Science, German, and Geographic Information Systems at Macalester College in Saint Paul, Minnesota. I studied abroad in Berlin and Vienna, where I took intensive German classes at the C1 level.

After my undergraduate studies, I gained valuable experience in IT through a front-end web development internship at Brandography in Minneapolis and a contract position at Apple Maps in California.

I moved to Norway in 2016 to pursue a master's degree in globalization, transnationalism and culture at NTNU in Trondheim. I had the opportunity there to intern for the US Department of State in the FSI School of Language Studies.

After I graduated, I moved to Japan for three years to teach English at the high-school level. I became proficient in Japanese and gained invaluable international experience.

I returned to Norway in summer of 2021 to further my education in IT. I studied applied computer science for one year at OsloMet.

After my studies, I began my career in low-code web development.
